Quinoa Salad

A delicious and hearty quinoa salad.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup quinoa
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 1/2 red onion, diced
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Rinse the quinoa under cold water.
  2. In a medium saucepan, bring the water to a boil. Add the quinoa, reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for about 15 minutes, or until the quinoa is tender and the water is absorbed.
  3. Remove the quinoa from heat and let it cool.
  4. In a large bowl, combine the cooled quinoa, cucumber, bell pepper, red onion, and parsley.
  5. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
  6. Pour the dressing over the quinoa salad and toss to combine.
  7. Serve immediately or refrigerate for later.
