Red Lentil Soup

A delicious and hearty red lentil soup.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup red lentils
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 carrot, diced
  • 1 celery stalk, diced
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 8 cups vegetable broth
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Rinse the red lentils and set aside.
  2. In a large pot, sauté the onion and garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add the carrot and celery, and cook for a few minutes.
  4. Stir in the cumin and paprika, and cook for another minute.
  5. Add the vegetable broth and bring to a boil.
  6. Reduce heat and simmer for 20-25 minutes, or until the lentils are tender.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
