Chicken Piccata

A delicious and hearty chicken piccata.

Ingredients

  • 4 chicken breasts
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 1/4 cup white wine
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped

Steps

  1. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper.
  2. Dredge the chicken in flour, shaking off excess.
  3. Beat the egg and dip the chicken in it.
  4. Coat the chicken in breadcrumbs.
  5. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  6. Cook the chicken until golden brown and cooked through, about 5-7 minutes per side.
  7. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside.
  8. In the same skillet, add the white wine and butter, and bring to a boil.
  9. Stir in the garlic and parsley.
  10. Pour the sauce over the chicken and serve immediately.
